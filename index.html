About us

We're an Ultimate Frisbee team based in Belfast.

When we meet:

Mondays: Training Victoria Park, 6-8pm

Wednesdays: Pick up at the top of Ormeau Park beside the inside tennis courts, 6.30-8.30pm

All you need is a bottle of water and some runners (cleats if you have them) and we'll provide the rest!

Beginners and experienced welcome!
